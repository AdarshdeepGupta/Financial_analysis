# 📈 ScootCharge Financial Model

A comprehensive **5-year financial model** developed for **ScootCharge
Solutions Pvt. Ltd.**, a pre-revenue clean-tech startup offering
foldable electric scooter charging docks for apartment complexes. The
model evaluates the company's financial feasibility through integrated
financial statements, capital budgeting, and investment analysis.


## Project Overview

This project demonstrates a complete financial planning and valuation
framework for a startup by combining market research, operational
assumptions, financial forecasting, and investment evaluation into a
single Excel model.

The workbook is designed to simulate the financial performance of
ScootCharge over a five-year period and can be adapted for scenario
analysis by modifying the assumptions sheet.



## Workbook Structure

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Sheet                       Description
  --------------------------- -------------------------------------------
  **01 Cover**                Project overview and company information

  **02 Industry**             Industry outlook, market need, and sector
                              analysis

  **03 Competitor**           Competitive landscape and product
                              comparison

  **04 Assumptions**          Master assumptions including pricing,
                              costs, and operational drivers

  **05 CapEx**                Capital expenditure planning and asset
                              requirements

  **06 Financing**            Capital structure, funding sources, and
                              financing assumptions

  **07 P&L**                  Five-year projected Income Statement

  **08 Balance Sheet**        Projected Balance Sheet

  **09 Cash Flow**            Projected Cash Flow Statement

  **10 Cap Budget**           Capital budgeting using Free Cash Flow to
                              Firm (FCFF)

  **11 Conclusion**           Financial KPIs and investment highlights
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
